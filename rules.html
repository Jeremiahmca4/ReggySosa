Official Tournament Rules

Notice: Failure to follow these rules may result in disqualification.

Format

  • Single Elimination
  • 1 Game per round
  • Crossplay ON (PS5 + Xbox)
  • NA‑East servers

Match Setup (World of CHEL)

  1. Both teams navigate to World of CHEL → Clubs → Play → Private Match.
  2. Apply the following match settings:
    • Game Type: Private Game
    • Password: (Generated for each match)
    • Server Selection: North America East
    • Replay Skipping: Yes
    • Period Length: 3 Minutes
    • Grudge Match Game: Off
    • Use Best Game Location: Relaxed
    • Side Selection: (Highest seed is Home)
  3. Highest seed chooses the Home side.
    • Example: Seed 2 vs Seed 5 → Seed 2 selects Home; Seed 5 selects Away.
    • Both players must pick the correct side or the game won’t match.
  4. After entering settings, both teams press Search simultaneously. The game will auto‑match you based on password and side selection.

Lag‑outs = DQ (no exceptions)

Gameplay Rules

  • Grudge Match setting = OFF
  • Lag‑outs = DQ (no replays)
  • No pause abuse or toxic behavior
  • If your opponent no‑shows after 10 minutes, ping a mod in ⁠❓・tourney‑questions

Reporting Scores

  • Winner must post a screenshot of the final score in 🧊・score‑submissions on Reggy Sosa’s Discord.
  • No screenshot = no win
